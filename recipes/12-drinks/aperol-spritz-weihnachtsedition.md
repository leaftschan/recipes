## Summary

Winter Aperol syrup with citrus and warm spices, served with prosecco.

## Ingredients

- 1 organic lemon, sliced
- 400 ml citrus juice (orange, blood orange, or grapefruit)
- 200 g sugar
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 5 cloves
- 5 allspice berries
- 200 ml aperol
- prosecco to serve

## Preparation

1. Simmer lemon slices, citrus juice, sugar, and spices for about 30 minutes until syrupy.
2. Remove from heat, stir in aperol.
3. Strain and bottle.
4. Serve with 2 cl syrup per glass and top with prosecco.
