## Summary

Classic two-ingredient bellini with fresh peach puree and chilled prosecco.

## Ingredients

### Peach puree

- 1.5 lb ripe peaches (about 4), peeled, pitted, and sliced

### For each cocktail

- 2 oz peach puree
- 4 oz chilled prosecco
- peach slice, optional garnish
- fresh mint sprig, optional garnish

## Preparation

1. Puree peeled peaches in a blender until smooth.
2. Pour 2 oz (1/4 cup) peach puree into a champagne flute.
3. Slowly top with 4 oz chilled prosecco and stir gently.
4. Garnish with peach slice and mint, then serve.
