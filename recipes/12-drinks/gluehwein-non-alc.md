## Summary

Non-alcoholic mulled fruit drink with warm winter spices.

## Ingredients

- 5 dl blackcurrant juice
- 5 dl cranberry juice
- 5 dl orange juice
- 2 organic oranges, sliced
- 2 cinnamon sticks
- 3 star anise
- 4 cloves
- freshly grated nutmeg
- optional: fresh cranberries

## Preparation

1. Slice oranges.
2. Combine all ingredients in a pot.
3. Heat gently until steaming, do not boil hard.
4. Let steep for a few minutes before serving.
