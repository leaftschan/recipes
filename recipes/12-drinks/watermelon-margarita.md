## Summary

Fresh watermelon margarita with lime and tequila, optionally infused with jalapeno.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup blanco tequila
- 1 small jalapeno, thinly sliced (optional)
- 5 cups cut seedless watermelon
- 1/2 cup fresh lime juice
- ice
- lime slices, optional garnish
- lime wedge and coarse salt for rims, optional

## Preparation

1. If desired, rub glass rims with lime and dip in coarse salt. Fill glasses with ice.
2. For a spicy version, combine tequila with half the jalapeno slices and infuse 15 to 30 minutes.
3. Blend watermelon, lime juice, and a handful of ice until smooth.
4. Pour watermelon mixture into prepared glasses.
5. Stir 2 tbsp tequila into each glass.
6. Taste, adjust with extra lime or watermelon if needed, garnish, and serve.
