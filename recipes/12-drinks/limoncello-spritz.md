## Summary

Simple sparkling aperitif with limoncello, prosecco, and soda.

## Ingredients

- 80 ml limoncello
- 400 ml prosecco
- 200 ml sparkling water
- ice cubes, optional
- lemon slices or zest, optional

## Preparation

1. For each glass, add 20 ml limoncello.
2. Add 100 ml prosecco and 50 ml sparkling water.
3. Add ice and lemon garnish if desired.
4. Serve immediately.
