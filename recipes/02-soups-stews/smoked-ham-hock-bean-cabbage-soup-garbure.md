## Summary

Traditional southwestern French soup with beans, vegetables, cabbage, and smoked ham hock.

## Ingredients

- 1 smoked ham hock
- 300 g dried white beans, soaked overnight
- 0.5 green cabbage, shredded
- 3 carrots, sliced
- 2 leeks, sliced
- 2 potatoes, diced
- 1 onion, halved
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2 l water or light stock
- 1 bouquet garni
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Simmer ham hock, soaked beans, onion, garlic, and bouquet garni in water for 1.5 hours.
2. Add carrots, leeks, potatoes, and cabbage.
3. Continue simmering 45 to 60 minutes until beans and vegetables are tender.
4. Remove ham hock, shred meat, and discard skin and bone.
5. Return meat to soup, season to taste, and serve hot.
