## Summary

A fragrant lamb ragout with quince, dried plums, cardamom, and chickpeas.

## Ingredients

- 500 g lamb, cubed
- 150 g onions, finely chopped
- 150 ml oil
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 small tomato, diced
- 1 garlic clove, chopped
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 250 ml water (plus extra as needed)
- 2 quinces (about 400 g total), peeled and cubed
- 100 g dried pitted plums (unsweetened)
- 50 g cooked chickpeas
- 4 cardamom pods (seeds only)
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 6 walnut halves, chopped
- juice of 1 lemon

## Preparation

1. Fry onions in oil in a large pot until golden.
2. Add lamb, salt, and brown well for about 10 minutes.
3. Add tomato, garlic, turmeric, black pepper, and cayenne.
4. Add water, cover, and simmer around 45 minutes until lamb is tender.
5. Prepare quince, cardamom seeds, and chopped walnuts.
6. Add quince, plums, chickpeas, cardamom, sugar, lemon juice, and walnuts.
7. Simmer covered over medium-low heat until quince is soft.
8. Continue on very low heat about 10 minutes for color and flavor.
9. Serve with basmati rice.

## Notes

- Add a little water during braising if needed.
- Balance sweetness/acidity at the end with sugar and lemon.
