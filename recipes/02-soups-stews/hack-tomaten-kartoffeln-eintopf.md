## Summary

Simple hearty one-pot stew with minced meat, potatoes, tomato, and pepper.

## Ingredients

- 250 g minced meat
- 2 potatoes, diced
- 1 can peeled tomatoes (pelati)
- onions, chopped
- garlic, chopped
- 1 pepperoni or bell pepper, diced
- bouillon
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Dice potatoes and fry briefly in a pot.
2. Add minced meat and brown.
3. Add onion and garlic and saute.
4. Add canned tomatoes.
5. Fill tomato can with water and add to the pot.
6. Add diced pepperoni/pepper.
7. Simmer around 30 minutes until potatoes are tender.
8. Season well with salt and pepper.
