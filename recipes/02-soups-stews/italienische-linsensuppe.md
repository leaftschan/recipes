## Summary

Hearty lentil soup with porcini, tomatoes, croutons, and parmesan.

## Ingredients

- 25 g dried porcini
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion
- 1 celery stalk
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 sprig sage
- 400 g quick-cooking brown lentils
- 200 g tomatoes
- 1 bay leaf
- 1.5 l vegetable broth
- 250 g day-old white bread
- 100 g grated parmesan
- salt

## Preparation

1. Soak porcini in warm water for about 15 minutes, then chop. Reserve soaking liquid.
2. Saute onion, celery, sage, and porcini in 2 tbsp olive oil. Add garlic briefly.
3. Add broth, lentils, tomatoes, bay leaf, and porcini liquid. Simmer about 30 minutes.
4. Dice bread, toss with remaining olive oil, and toast in the oven until golden.
5. Season soup with salt, serve with croutons and parmesan.
