## Summary

Hearty country soup with green cabbage, potatoes, and sausage.

## Ingredients

- 1 small green cabbage, shredded
- 400 g smoked sausage, sliced
- 3 potatoes, diced
- 2 carrots, sliced
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1.5 l chicken or vegetable stock
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 bay leaf
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Heat oil in a large pot and cook onion and carrots for 5 minutes.
2. Add garlic and sausage and cook 2 to 3 minutes.
3. Add cabbage, potatoes, stock, and bay leaf.
4. Bring to a boil, then simmer 45 to 60 minutes until vegetables are tender.
5. Season with salt and pepper and serve hot.
