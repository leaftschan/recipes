## Summary

Tuscan bread-tomato soup with soffritto, basil, and olive oil.

## Ingredients

- 300 g day-old ciabatta
- 1 can San Marzano tomatoes
- 1 celery stalk
- 1 carrot
- 1 onion
- 3 garlic cloves
- 200 ml olive oil, divided
- 15 basil leaves
- salt
- black pepper
- water as needed

## Preparation

1. Finely chop onion, carrot, and celery; saute in half the olive oil until golden.
2. Add tomatoes, salt, and half a can of water. Simmer for 20 minutes.
3. Remove crust from bread, tear into pieces, soak briefly in water, then squeeze.
4. Add bread, pressed garlic, and sliced basil to the tomato base.
5. Simmer 5 more minutes, adding a little water if too thick.
6. Stir in the remaining olive oil, season, and serve warm.
