## Summary

Baked eggplant-potato casserole with tomato sauce, feta, and warm spices.

## Ingredients

- 3 eggplants
- potatoes (amount not specified), boiled with skin
- 1 chili
- 1 onion
- garlic
- tomato sauce or canned tomatoes
- tomato paste
- 500 g minced beef (optional if preparing vegetarian version)
- cumin
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- rosemary
- feta
- oil for greasing and roasting
- salt and pepper

## Preparation

1. Prepare tomato sauce with chili, tomato paste, onion, garlic, spices, and optionally minced beef.
2. Simmer sauce about 1 hour.
3. Slice eggplants into about 1 cm rounds, salt, and rest 30 minutes.
4. Par-cook potatoes about 15 minutes with skin, then drain and slice thickly.
5. Pre-roast or pan-fry eggplant slices.
6. Grease casserole dish.
7. Layer potato slices, salt lightly, then eggplant.
8. Crumble feta between layers.
9. Pour sauce over and bake at 200 C for about 45 minutes.
