## Summary

Layered baked aubergine with tomato sauce, mozzarella, parmesan, and basil.

## Ingredients

- 2 onions, finely diced
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 l passata
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 tsp salt
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 500 g aubergines
- 200 ml neutral oil for frying
- 2 mozzarella balls (about 120 g each)
- 150 g grated parmesan
- 1 bunch basil

## Preparation

1. Saute onions and lightly crushed garlic in olive oil until soft, then remove garlic.
2. Add passata, bay leaf, and salt. Simmer until reduced and thick, then remove bay leaf.
3. Slice aubergines about 0.5 cm thick, fry in batches until golden, and drain.
4. Heat oven to 180 C (fan).
5. Layer sauce, aubergine, mozzarella, parmesan, and basil in a baking dish. Repeat.
6. Finish with parmesan and bake about 40 minutes until deeply golden.
7. Rest briefly before serving.
