## Summary

Layered potato and spinach casserole with feta bechamel.

## Ingredients

### Béchamel

- 30 g butter
- 30 g flour
- 250 ml milk
- 250 ml vegetable broth
- 200 g feta (100 g for sauce, 100 g for topping)
- salt, pepper, nutmeg
- lemon juice

### Filling

- 3 onions
- 3 garlic cloves
- 450 g leaf spinach
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1.2 kg waxy potatoes

## Preparation

1. For béchamel, melt butter, stir in flour, and cook briefly.
2. Add milk and broth while stirring; simmer about 5 minutes.
3. Melt in about 100 g feta and season with salt, pepper, nutmeg, and lemon juice.
4. Chop onion and garlic.
5. Wash and dry spinach.
6. Saute onion and garlic in olive oil, add spinach, and wilt down.
7. Season spinach with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
8. Preheat oven to 175 C.
9. Peel and thinly slice potatoes.
10. Layer a thin potato layer in greased dish and season.
11. Add spinach and half the béchamel.
12. Cover with remaining potatoes and béchamel.
13. Crumble remaining feta on top.
14. Bake about 60 minutes.

## Notes

- Cover loosely with foil if top browns too quickly.
- Rest 10 minutes before serving.
