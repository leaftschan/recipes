## Summary

Savory spinach muffins baked on crisp potato roesti bases.

## Ingredients

### Roesti base

- 500 g waxy potatoes, peeled and coarsely grated
- 1 tbsp neutral oil
- 1 tbsp butter, melted
- salt and pepper

### Spinach muffin filling

- 250 g spinach (fresh or thawed frozen, squeezed dry)
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 3 eggs
- 120 ml cream or milk
- 120 g feta, crumbled
- 60 g grated hard cheese
- nutmeg
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 200 C.
2. Grease muffin tin well.
3. Mix grated potatoes with oil, butter, salt, and pepper.
4. Press potato mixture into muffin cups to form compact bases.
5. Prebake bases for 12 to 15 minutes until starting to brown.
6. Saute onion and garlic briefly; add spinach and cook off moisture.
7. Whisk eggs with cream, feta, grated cheese, nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
8. Fold in spinach mixture.
9. Fill muffin cups over prebaked roesti bases.
10. Bake another 15 to 18 minutes until set and golden.

## Notes

- Serve warm with salad or yogurt dip.
- These reheat well and work for meal prep.
