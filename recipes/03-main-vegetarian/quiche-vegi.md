## Summary

Vegetarian quiche with leek, onion, and cherry tomatoes.

## Ingredients

- 1 wholegrain (dinkel) pie dough
- 250 g creme fraiche
- 4 eggs
- 1 to 2 onions
- 1 leek
- 2 handfuls cherry tomatoes
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 180 C fan.
2. Line quiche dish with dough.
3. Finely slice onions.
4. Trim and wash leek, halve lengthwise, and slice very thinly.
5. Mix onion and leek and distribute on dough.
6. Whisk eggs with creme fraiche, season well, and pour over vegetables.
7. Halve cherry tomatoes and press cut-side up into the custard.
8. Bake around 35 minutes until set and golden.
