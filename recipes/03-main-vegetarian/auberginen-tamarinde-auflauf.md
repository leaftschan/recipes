## Summary

Baked eggplant and potato casserole with tangy tamarind-tomato sauce and feta.

## Ingredients

### Vegetables

- 2 large eggplants
- 500 g potatoes
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp olive oil

### Tamarind tomato sauce

- 1 can peeled tomatoes
- 1 to 2 tbsp tamarind paste
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 pinch chili flakes
- salt and black pepper

### Finish

- 150 g feta, crumbled
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley

## Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 200 C.
2. Slice eggplants into rounds, salt lightly, and let rest 15 minutes.
3. Slice potatoes thinly and parboil 8 to 10 minutes.
4. Saute onion and garlic in olive oil.
5. Add tomatoes, tamarind paste, cumin, cinnamon, chili, salt, and pepper.
6. Simmer sauce 10 minutes and adjust tanginess.
7. Pat eggplant dry and roast or pan-sear until lightly browned.
8. Layer potatoes and eggplant in a greased casserole dish.
9. Pour tamarind tomato sauce over layers.
10. Top with feta.
11. Bake 30 to 35 minutes.
12. Finish with parsley.
