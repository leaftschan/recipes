## Summary

Roasted aubergines glazed with a sweet-salty miso sauce.

## Ingredients

### Main

- 2 large aubergines
- 2 tbsp neutral oil

### Miso glaze

- 2 tbsp white miso
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp maple syrup or honey
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp water

### To finish

- 2 spring onions, sliced
- 1 tsp sesame seeds

## Preparation

1. Heat oven to 220 C.
2. Halve aubergines and score flesh in a cross pattern.
3. Brush with oil and roast cut side up for 15 minutes.
4. Mix all glaze ingredients until smooth.
5. Brush glaze onto aubergines and roast for 8 to 12 more minutes.
6. Finish with spring onions and sesame.
