## Summary

Flexible Swiss fruit tart base with cream-egg custard and seasonal fruit.

## Ingredients

### Base and custard

- 1 puff pastry
- grated hazelnuts
- 2 eggs
- 2 dl cream
- 1 dl milk
- cinnamon
- vanilla sugar

### Choose one fruit option

- 600 g rhubarb stalks
- or 4 apples
- or 800 g plums
- or 800 g apricots

### Sweetening

- sugar

## Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 220 C top-bottom heat or 200 C fan.
2. Place pastry in tart pan and prick densely with a fork.
3. Scatter grated hazelnuts over base.
4. Prepare fruit: peel rhubarb/apples, or halve and pit plums/apricots.
5. Arrange fruit on tart and sprinkle with about 2 tbsp sugar.
6. Mix eggs, cream, milk, cinnamon, and vanilla sugar.
7. Pour custard over fruit and bake immediately.
8. Bake about 35 minutes.
