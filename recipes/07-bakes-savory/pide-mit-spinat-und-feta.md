## Summary

Baked pide filled with spinach, feta, egg, and warm spices.

## Ingredients

- 1 onion
- 500 g thawed frozen leaf spinach, well squeezed
- 200 g feta
- 3 eggs
- cumin
- nutmeg
- salt and black pepper
- 500 g yeast dough

## Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 200 C fan.
2. Finely chop onion.
3. Mix onion, squeezed spinach, crumbled feta, eggs, cumin, and nutmeg.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Divide dough and fill to make about 6 to 8 pide.
6. Bake about 30 minutes.
