## Summary

Long-fermented focaccia with cherry tomatoes, olive oil, and oregano.

## Ingredients

- 500 g tipo 00 flour
- 250 ml cold water
- 2 g fresh yeast
- 20 g salt
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 50 ml olive oil
- 250 g cherry tomatoes
- coarse salt
- oregano

## Preparation

1. Dissolve salt in water. Crumble yeast into flour.
2. Add water gradually and knead, then knead in sugar and olive oil.
3. Knead 10 minutes, rest 2 hours at room temperature, then chill 24 hours.
4. Bring dough to room temperature for 2 hours.
5. Press dough onto an oiled tray and dimple with fingers.
6. Halve tomatoes, squeeze some juice over dough, press tomatoes into dough, proof 30 minutes.
7. Top with salt, oregano, and olive oil. Bake at 180 C for about 25 minutes.
