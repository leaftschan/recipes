## Summary

Thin-crust tray pizza with tomato sauce, cheese, and assorted toppings.

## Ingredients

- pizza dough from about 1 kg flour (for 3 to 4 pizzas)
- tomato sauce
- Italian herbs
- onions
- olives
- capers
- anchovies
- artichokes
- salami
- pizza cheese
- fresh basil

## Preparation

1. Preheat oven to maximum heat with top and bottom heat.
2. Roll pizza dough as thin as possible.
3. Transfer to baking-paper-lined tray.
4. Spread with tomato sauce and season with Italian herbs.
5. Add onions, olives, capers, anchovies, artichokes, and salami.
6. Bake about 10 minutes.
7. Add cheese and continue baking until melted.
8. Finish with fresh basil.
