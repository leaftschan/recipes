## Summary

Upside-down savory tart with caramelized pearl onions and balsamic.

## Ingredients

- 500 g pearl onions, peeled
- 1 sheet puff pastry
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 1 tsp fresh thyme
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Heat oven to 200 C.
2. Cook onions in butter and oil until lightly golden.
3. Add brown sugar, balsamic, thyme, salt, and pepper and cook until syrupy.
4. Arrange onions in an ovenproof skillet and top with puff pastry.
5. Bake 25 to 30 minutes until pastry is puffed and golden.
6. Rest 5 minutes, invert carefully, and serve warm.
