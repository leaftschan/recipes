## Summary

White pizza with mozzarella, rosemary, and prosciutto.

## Ingredients

- 1 kg tipo 00 flour
- 600 ml water
- 30 g salt
- 3 g dry yeast
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 50 ml olive oil
- 4 mozzarella balls
- rosemary
- 16 slices prosciutto
- extra olive oil

## Preparation

1. Dissolve salt in water. Mix yeast with sugar and a little water.
2. Combine flour, yeast mix, and salted water, then knead in olive oil.
3. Knead about 10 minutes, rest 1 hour at room temperature.
4. Divide into 8 dough balls and proof 6 to 7 hours covered.
5. Stretch into pizzas, top with mozzarella, rosemary, and olive oil.
6. Bake at highest oven temperature until golden.
7. Top with prosciutto after baking.
