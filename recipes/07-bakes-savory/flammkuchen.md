## Summary

Crisp flammkuchen with creme fraiche, onion, and bacon.

## Ingredients

- 2 flammkuchen dough sheets
- 500 g creme fraiche
- 2 packs bacon cubes
- 3 onions
- salt
- black pepper

## Preparation

1. Preheat oven as hot as possible on fan setting.
2. Spread dough with creme fraiche.
3. Season lightly with salt and pepper.
4. Halve onions, slice thinly, separate layers, and scatter on top.
5. Add bacon cubes.
6. Bake about 10 minutes until crisp.
