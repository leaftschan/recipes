## Summary

Savory tart layered with Dijon mustard, cheese, and mixed tomatoes.

## Ingredients

- 1 sheet puff pastry
- 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 100 g grated Gruyere or Comte
- 600 g mixed tomatoes, sliced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp thyme leaves
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Heat oven to 200 C and line a tart pan with pastry.
2. Prick base with a fork and spread Dijon mustard over it.
3. Sprinkle grated cheese.
4. Arrange tomato slices in overlapping rings.
5. Drizzle with olive oil and season with thyme, salt, and pepper.
6. Bake 30 to 35 minutes until pastry is crisp and golden.
7. Cool slightly before slicing.
