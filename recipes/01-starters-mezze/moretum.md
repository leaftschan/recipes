## Summary

Ancient Roman herb-cheese spread made by pounding ingredients in a mortar.

## Ingredients

- 150 g finely grated pecorino
- 3 to 5 garlic cloves, peeled
- 20 coriander leaves
- a few rue leaves (or a little wild garlic as substitute)
- a little celery greens
- 20 ml balsamic vinegar
- 20 ml olive oil
- pinch of salt

## Preparation

1. Pound garlic with salt in a mortar.
2. Add herbs and celery greens and crush to a coarse paste.
3. Add grated pecorino and work into the herb paste.
4. Add vinegar and olive oil gradually while pounding until creamy.
5. Shape or spoon into a bowl and serve with bread.
