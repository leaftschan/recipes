## Summary

Thinly sliced raw beef filet with a light Cipriani-style sauce.

## Ingredients

- 400 g beef filet
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 1/2 tsp mild mustard
- 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 40 ml neutral oil (for example peanut oil)
- salt

## Preparation

1. Chill beef briefly so it is easier to slice, then cut paper-thin slices across the grain.
2. Arrange slices on cold plates and press gently into an even layer.
3. Whisk egg yolk, lemon juice, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and oil into a light emulsion.
4. Season to taste and spoon the sauce thinly over the beef.
5. Serve immediately.
