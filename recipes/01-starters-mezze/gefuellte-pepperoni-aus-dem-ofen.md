## Summary

Roasted pointed peppers filled with spiced rice, vegetables, and feta.

## Ingredients

- wild rice and/or risotto rice
- carrots, finely chopped
- chili, finely chopped
- onions, finely chopped
- bouillon powder and water
- ground cinnamon
- parsley (prezzemolo), chopped
- raisins
- pointed peppers (Spitzpeperoni)
- feta, crumbled
- oil for tray and peppers

## Preparation

1. Saute onions and chili in a little oil.
2. Add chopped carrots and cook briefly.
3. Add rice, then enough bouillon to cook it like a pilaf.
4. Season with cinnamon and any additional spices you prefer.
5. Simmer until rice is cooked and only a little liquid remains.
6. Stir in raisins and parsley.
7. Heat oven to 120 C.
8. Halve and deseed pointed peppers, oil lightly, place on a tray.
9. Fill peppers with rice mixture and top with feta.
10. Bake at least 40 minutes, ideally about 1 hour.
