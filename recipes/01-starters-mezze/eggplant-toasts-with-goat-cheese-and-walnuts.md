## Summary

Roasted eggplant toasts with creamy goat cheese and crunchy walnuts.

## Ingredients

- 1 baguette, sliced
- 1 eggplant, diced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 150 g goat cheese
- 50 g walnuts, toasted and chopped
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp chopped thyme or parsley
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Heat oven to 200 C.
2. Toss eggplant with olive oil, salt, and pepper and roast 20 minutes until tender.
3. Toast baguette slices until crisp.
4. Spread goat cheese on each toast and top with roasted eggplant.
5. Add walnuts, a little honey, and herbs.
6. Serve warm.
