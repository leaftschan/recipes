## Summary

Silky, rich mousse flavored with shallot and a splash of brandy.

## Ingredients

- 450 g chicken livers, cleaned
- 1 shallot, finely chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 120 g unsalted butter, divided
- 2 tbsp brandy or cognac
- 4 tbsp heavy cream
- 1 tsp thyme leaves
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Melt half the butter and soften shallot and garlic for 3 minutes.
2. Add chicken livers and cook until just pink inside.
3. Add brandy and cook 30 seconds.
4. Transfer to a processor with cream, thyme, remaining butter, salt, and pepper.
5. Blend until very smooth, then pass through a fine sieve if desired.
6. Chill at least 2 hours before serving.
