## Summary

Poached chicken slices with creamy tuna-caper sauce, best after marinating.

## Ingredients

### Chicken

- 1 chicken breast
- about 500 ml bouillon

### Sauce

- 1 small can tuna in oil, drained
- 1 egg yolk, room temperature
- about 1.5 dl sunflower oil, room temperature
- 1/2 tsp honey mustard
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- salt
- a little poaching broth as needed

### Finish

- capers

## Preparation

1. Bring bouillon to a boil.
2. Slice chicken thinly and poach gently in bouillon for about 10 minutes.
3. Let chicken cool in the broth for tenderness.
4. For mayo base, whisk egg yolk and add oil drop by drop until thick.
5. Mix in lemon juice, mustard, and a little salt.
6. Add drained tuna and blend or mash into the mayo.
7. Loosen sauce with a little poaching broth until creamy.
8. Arrange chicken slices in a shallow dish, cover with sauce, and top with capers.
9. Chill and let flavors combine before serving.
