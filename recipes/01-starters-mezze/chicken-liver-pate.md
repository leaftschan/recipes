## Summary

Smooth chicken liver pate with butter, shallot, and a touch of brandy.

## Ingredients

- 450 g chicken livers, cleaned
- 1 shallot, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 140 g unsalted butter, divided
- 2 tbsp brandy or cognac
- 1 tsp thyme
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Melt half the butter and saute shallot and garlic until soft.
2. Add livers, season, and cook until just pink inside.
3. Add brandy and reduce briefly.
4. Blend with remaining butter and thyme until very smooth.
5. Taste for seasoning and transfer to ramekins.
6. Chill until firm before serving.
