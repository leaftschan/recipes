## Summary

Quick fish spread with sardines, cream cheese, lemon, and herbs.

## Ingredients

- 2 cans sardines in olive oil, drained
- 120 g cream cheese
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp chives, chopped
- black pepper

## Preparation

1. Mash sardines with cream cheese.
2. Mix in lemon juice, Dijon, chives, and pepper.
3. Chill 15 minutes if possible.
4. Serve with crackers or toasted bread.
