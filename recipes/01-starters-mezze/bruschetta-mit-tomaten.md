## Summary

Classic bruschetta with toasted ciabatta, marinated tomatoes, basil, and olive oil.

## Ingredients

- 4 slices ciabatta
- 8 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, divided
- 1 garlic clove
- 2 large ripe tomatoes
- 1/2 onion, finely diced
- 10 basil leaves
- sea salt
- black pepper

## Preparation

1. Toast ciabatta slowly in a pan with 4 tbsp olive oil until golden on both sides. Season lightly with salt and pepper.
2. Rub warm bread slices with the garlic clove.
3. Dice tomatoes, salt lightly, and mix with a little olive oil.
4. Mix in onion and 4 roughly torn basil leaves. Let sit for 5 minutes.
5. Spoon tomato mixture onto bread, finish with remaining basil and a thin drizzle of olive oil.
