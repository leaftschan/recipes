## Summary

Warm or room-temperature pepper salad with chickpeas, herbs, and lemon.

## Ingredients

- 4 peppers (yellow/red)
- 1 small can chickpeas
- olive oil
- lemon juice
- 1 garlic clove, pressed
- rosemary
- oregano
- salt
- a little bouillon (optional, for dressing)

## Preparation

1. Set oven to grill mode, around 180 C.
2. Rinse chickpeas very well and let them dry in a sieve.
3. Quarter peppers and place skin-side up on a baking-paper-lined tray.
4. Grill until skin is blackened.
5. Remove, cool slightly, peel skin, and transfer peppers to a bowl.
6. Mix dressing from about 4 tbsp olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and a little bouillon if desired.
7. Add chickpeas and herbs, then toss with peppers.
8. Let salad sit so flavors can combine.

## Notes

- Best after resting for at least 20 to 30 minutes.
- Works as mezze, side dish, or light lunch.
