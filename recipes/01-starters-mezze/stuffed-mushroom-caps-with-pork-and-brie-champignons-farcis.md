## Summary

Baked mushroom caps filled with savory pork and melted Brie.

## Ingredients

- 18 large button mushrooms
- 250 g ground pork
- 1 shallot, finely chopped
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 100 g Brie, cubed
- 1 tbsp parsley, chopped
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Heat oven to 190 C and lightly oil a baking dish.
2. Remove mushroom stems and chop them finely.
3. Cook shallot, garlic, and chopped stems in olive oil for 3 to 4 minutes.
4. Add pork, season, and cook until just done.
5. Stir in parsley and fill mushroom caps with the mixture.
6. Top each with Brie and bake 15 to 20 minutes until mushrooms are tender.
