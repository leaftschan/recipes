## Summary

Crisp salt-cod fritters with herbs and a light chili kick.

## Ingredients

- 250 g salted cod, soaked and cooked
- 150 g all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 egg
- 120 ml water
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp parsley, chopped
- 1 small chili, minced
- oil for frying
- black pepper

## Preparation

1. Flake cooked cod and remove bones and skin.
2. Mix flour and baking powder in a bowl.
3. Add egg and water to make a thick batter.
4. Stir in cod, onion, garlic, parsley, chili, and pepper.
5. Heat oil to 170 to 180 C.
6. Drop spoonfuls of batter into oil and fry until deep golden.
7. Drain on paper and serve hot.
