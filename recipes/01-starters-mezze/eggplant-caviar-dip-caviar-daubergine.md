## Summary

Smoky roasted eggplant dip with garlic, olive oil, and lemon.

## Ingredients

- 2 large eggplants
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp tahini (optional)
- 1 tbsp parsley, chopped
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Heat oven to 220 C. Prick eggplants and roast 30 to 35 minutes until very soft.
2. Cool slightly, then scoop out flesh and drain excess liquid.
3. Blend eggplant with garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, and tahini until smooth.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Chill and top with parsley before serving.
