## Summary

Sweet-sour marinated zucchini with onion, vinegar, and mint, served at room temperature.

## Ingredients

- 3 medium zucchini
- 1 onion, thinly sliced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- oil for frying zucchini
- 3 tbsp white wine vinegar
- 1 tsp sugar
- salt and black pepper
- 1 tbsp fresh mint, chopped
- optional: 1 garlic clove, finely sliced

## Preparation

1. Slice zucchini lengthwise into thin strips.
2. Salt lightly and let rest 10 minutes, then pat dry.
3. Fry zucchini in batches until lightly golden; set aside.
4. In a clean pan, heat olive oil and soften onion (and garlic if using).
5. Add vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper; simmer briefly.
6. Layer zucchini and onion marinade in a shallow dish.
7. Sprinkle with mint.
8. Let marinate at least 30 minutes before serving.

## Notes

- Best after a few hours at room temperature or chilled overnight.
- Serve with bread, grilled meat, or as part of a mezze spread.
