## Summary

Creamy cucumber-yogurt dip with mint and optional garlic.

## Ingredients

- 500 g natural yogurt
- 1 cucumber
- olive oil
- salt
- black pepper
- mint, finely chopped
- optional: garlic

## Preparation

1. Grate cucumber and mix with salt.
2. Place in a sieve and let drain for about 30 minutes.
3. Squeeze drained cucumber by hand to remove extra liquid.
4. Mix cucumber with yogurt, olive oil, and pepper.
5. Adjust salt and fold in finely chopped mint.
6. Add garlic if desired.

## Notes

- Let it rest briefly in the fridge before serving.
- If you want a thicker texture, use Greek yogurt.
