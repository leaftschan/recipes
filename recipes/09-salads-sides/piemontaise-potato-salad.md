## Summary

Creamy French potato salad with tomatoes, eggs, pickles, and herbs.

## Ingredients

- 1 kg waxy potatoes
- 3 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 4 cornichons, finely chopped
- 1 tbsp capers
- 3 tbsp mayonnaise
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Boil potatoes in salted water until tender. Cool slightly, peel if desired, and dice.
2. Whisk mayonnaise, Dijon, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
3. Combine potatoes, eggs, tomatoes, cornichons, and capers.
4. Fold in dressing and parsley.
5. Chill 20 to 30 minutes before serving.
