## Summary

Soft poached leeks dressed with a sharp Dijon and shallot vinaigrette.

## Ingredients

- 6 medium leeks
- 1 shallot, minced
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Trim leeks, wash well, and cut into long sections.
2. Simmer in salted water for 12 to 15 minutes until tender.
3. Drain and let cool to warm or room temperature.
4. Whisk shallot, Dijon, vinegar, salt, and pepper, then whisk in olive oil.
5. Arrange leeks and spoon vinaigrette over them.
6. Finish with parsley and serve.
