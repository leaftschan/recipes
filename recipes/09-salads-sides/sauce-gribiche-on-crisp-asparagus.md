## Summary

Crisp asparagus served with a tangy egg-and-caper gribiche sauce.

## Ingredients

- 600 g green asparagus, trimmed
- 2 hard-boiled eggs, finely chopped
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp capers, chopped
- 1 tbsp cornichons, chopped
- 1 tbsp parsley, chopped
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Blanch asparagus in salted boiling water for 3 to 5 minutes until crisp-tender.
2. Cool briefly and drain.
3. Whisk Dijon and vinegar with salt and pepper, then whisk in olive oil.
4. Fold in chopped eggs, capers, cornichons, and parsley.
5. Spoon sauce over asparagus and serve.
