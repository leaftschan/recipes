## Summary

Warm balsamic lentils with optional burrata and sauteed pumpkin.

## Ingredients

- 250 g lentils (Beluga or Le Puy)
- 1 onion, finely diced
- 300 ml vegetable broth
- 1 tsp thyme
- salt and black pepper

### Marinade

- 70 ml balsamic vinegar
- 1 tbsp mustard
- 1 tbsp honey
- 2 tbsp olive oil

## Preparation

1. Cook lentils in broth until tender.
2. Saute onion in olive oil, season with thyme, salt, and pepper.
3. Add drained lentils, deglaze with half the balsamic, and simmer briefly.
4. Whisk remaining balsamic with mustard, honey, and olive oil.
5. Toss lentils with dressing and serve warm.
