## Summary

Green salad topped with warm toasted goat cheese rounds.

## Ingredients

- 1 small baguette, sliced
- 8 goat cheese rounds
- 150 g mixed greens
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp honey
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Heat oven to 200 C and place baguette slices on a tray.
2. Top each slice with a goat cheese round and bake 8 to 10 minutes until warm and lightly golden.
3. Whisk Dijon, vinegar, honey, salt, pepper, and olive oil for the dressing.
4. Toss greens with dressing and divide onto plates.
5. Top each plate with warm goat cheese toasts and serve immediately.
