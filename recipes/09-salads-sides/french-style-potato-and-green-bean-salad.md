## Summary

Warm potato and green bean salad with a sharp Dijon shallot vinaigrette.

## Ingredients

- 900 g waxy potatoes
- 340 g green beans, trimmed
- 1 small shallot, finely minced
- 2 tbsp white wine vinegar
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 5 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Boil potatoes in salted water until tender, then drain and slice while warm.
2. Blanch green beans in salted boiling water for 4 to 5 minutes, then cool briefly.
3. Whisk shallot, vinegar, Dijon, salt, and pepper.
4. Whisk in olive oil to form a vinaigrette.
5. Toss warm potatoes and green beans with vinaigrette and parsley.
6. Serve slightly warm or at room temperature.
