## Summary

Creamy cucumber salad with yogurt, dill, and apple vinegar.

## Ingredients

- 1 cucumber, halved, deseeded, sliced
- 2 tbsp apple vinegar
- 3 tbsp rapeseed oil
- 150 g Greek yogurt
- salt
- black pepper
- lots of dill, finely chopped

## Preparation

1. Prepare cucumber and slice thinly.
2. Mix cucumber with vinegar, oil, yogurt, dill, salt, and pepper.
3. Let rest briefly before serving.
