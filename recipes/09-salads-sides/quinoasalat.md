## Summary

Colorful quinoa salad with mango, cucumber, tomato, greens, mint, and feta.

## Ingredients

- 200 g quinoa
- 1 mango
- 1 cucumber
- 3 tomatoes
- 1 pepperoni or pepper
- 150 g lamb's lettuce
- mint
- 150 g feta
- olive oil
- apple vinegar
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Rinse quinoa.
2. Cook with double the amount of water for about 10 minutes.
3. Dice mango, cucumber, tomatoes, and pepperoni/pepper.
4. Wash and dry lamb's lettuce.
5. Chop mint and cube feta.
6. Drain quinoa and let cool slightly.
7. Mix quinoa with remaining ingredients.
8. Dress with olive oil, apple vinegar, salt, and pepper.
