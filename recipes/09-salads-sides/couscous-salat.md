## Summary

Fresh couscous salad with cucumber, tomato, olives, feta, and mint.

## Ingredients

- 250 g couscous
- 5 dl strong bouillon
- 2 cucumbers, diced
- tomatoes, diced
- olives, halved
- feta, diced
- mint, chopped
- olive oil
- lemon juice

## Preparation

1. Bring bouillon to a boil and pour over couscous.
2. Let couscous absorb liquid and fluff with a fork.
3. Dice cucumbers and tomatoes, halve olives, and cube feta.
4. Mix all components together.
5. Fold in chopped mint.
6. Dress with lemon juice and olive oil.
