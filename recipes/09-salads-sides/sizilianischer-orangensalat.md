## Summary

Fresh Sicilian orange salad with fennel, onion, olives, and citrus vinaigrette.

## Ingredients

- 2 oranges
- 1 fennel bulb
- 2 small onions
- 2 tbsp black olives
- 1 tsp fresh rosemary, chopped
- 1 tbsp white balsamic vinegar
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- salt
- black pepper

## Preparation

1. Peel oranges thickly and remove all white pith. Catch any juice.
2. Slice oranges and arrange on a platter.
3. Dice fennel heart finely and slice onions very thin.
4. Whisk reserved orange juice with vinegar, olive oil, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
5. Toss fennel in part of the dressing and scatter over oranges.
6. Add onions and olives, then spoon over remaining dressing.
