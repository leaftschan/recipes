## Summary

Crispy oven-roasted potatoes with rosemary and salt.

## Ingredients

- 1 kg small waxy potatoes
- rosemary leaves
- oil
- salt

## Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 190 C fan.
2. Wash and quarter potatoes.
3. Toss in a large bowl with a generous splash of oil and enough salt.
4. Strip rosemary leaves and mix in.
5. Spread on a baking-paper-lined tray.
6. Roast about 40 minutes until crisp and browned.
