## Summary

Tender white asparagus with browned butter, capers, lemon, and egg.

## Ingredients

- 1 kg white asparagus, peeled
- 2 eggs, hard-boiled and chopped
- 60 g butter
- 1 tbsp capers
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Cook asparagus in salted simmering water until just tender.
2. Melt butter in a pan and cook until lightly browned and nutty.
3. Stir in capers and lemon juice.
4. Arrange asparagus on a platter and spoon over brown butter.
5. Finish with chopped egg, parsley, pepper, and a little salt.
