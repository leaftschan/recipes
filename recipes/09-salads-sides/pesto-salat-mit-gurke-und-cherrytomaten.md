## Summary

Pasta salad with pesto, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, and pine nuts.

## Ingredients

- 250 g pasta
- 1 cucumber, halved and sliced
- 500 g cherry tomatoes, halved
- plenty of pesto
- pine nuts
- mozzarella balls

## Preparation

1. Cook pasta and drain.
2. Mix warm pasta with pesto and pine nuts.
3. Let pasta cool.
4. Add cucumber, tomatoes, and mozzarella.
5. Toss and chill if desired.
