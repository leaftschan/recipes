## Summary

Pan-seared fennel glazed with honey and balsamic.

## Ingredients

- 2 fennel bulbs
- olive oil
- about 1 tbsp honey
- balsamic vinegar
- salt

## Preparation

1. Cut fennel into eighths.
2. Sear in olive oil until lightly browned.
3. Add honey and cook briefly.
4. Deglaze with balsamic vinegar.
5. Season with salt and cook until fennel is tender.

## Notes

- Serve warm as side dish.
- Add black pepper or chili flakes for more contrast.
