## Summary

Lentil salad with carrot, celery, vinegar, oil, and fresh herbs.

## Ingredients

- 100 g lentils
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 carrot, small dice
- celery stalk, small dice
- olive oil
- vinegar
- bouillon powder
- herbs (parsley, dill)
- optional: garlic

## Preparation

1. Rinse lentils and cook without salt with bay leaf.
2. Dice carrot and celery finely.
3. Drain cooked lentils, keeping a little cooking liquid.
4. Season lentils with bouillon powder.
5. Mix in carrot and celery.
6. Add olive oil and vinegar.
7. Add herbs and garlic if desired.

## Notes

- Best when slightly warm or after brief resting time.
- Add lemon juice for extra brightness.
