## Summary

Soft saffron flatbread with sesame topping, with an optional savory yogurt spread variation.

## Ingredients

### Dough

- saffron powder
- 250 g flour
- 75 ml lukewarm milk
- 80 ml water
- 1/4 cube yeast
- 1 pinch salt

### Topping

- egg yolk
- sesame
- optional: a little sugar

### Alternative topping

- 1 to 2 onions
- 1 tbsp savory (bohnenkraut)
- 1 tbsp nigella seeds
- 100 g yogurt

## Preparation

1. Prepare yeast dough with saffron and let rise 1 to 2 hours.
2. Shape into small flat rounds and let rise again.
3. Score a diamond pattern on top.
4. Brush with egg yolk and sprinkle sesame.
5. Optionally add a little sugar.
6. Bake at 200 C for about 15 minutes.
7. Optional variation: blend onion, savory, nigella, and yogurt and spread instead of egg-yolk-sesame topping.
