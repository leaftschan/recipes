## Summary

Long-fermented pizza dough using lievito madre.

## Ingredients

- 10 g lievito madre
- 1 kg tipo 00 flour, divided
- 30 g salt
- 600 ml cold water, divided
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 50 ml olive oil

## Preparation

1. Mix lievito madre with 100 g flour, sugar, and about 50 ml water. Ferment 2 hours.
2. Dissolve salt in 550 ml cold water.
3. Mix 900 g flour with the starter, then add salted water gradually.
4. Knead until smooth, then knead in olive oil.
5. Rest 3 hours at room temperature.
6. Divide into 8 dough balls, chill covered for 21 hours, then rest 3 hours at room temperature.
7. Shape pizzas, top as desired, and bake at maximum heat.
