## Summary

Basic enriched yeast dough for braid bread and other baked goods.

## Ingredients

- 500 g braid flour (or other flour)
- 100 g butter, room temperature
- 1/2 cube fresh yeast
- 2.5 dl milk, lukewarm
- 1 tbsp sugar
- salt

## Preparation

1. Mix milk, sugar, and yeast; let stand at least 15 minutes.
2. Mix flour and salt in a bowl.
3. Pour in yeast-milk mixture.
4. Add butter in small pieces.
5. Knead well until smooth dough forms.
6. Let rise 1 to 2 hours in a warm place.
7. Shape braid (or desired form) and let rise again.
8. Bake at 200 C for about 35 minutes.

## Notes

- For glossy braid, brush with egg wash before baking.
- Bake time varies by shape and size.
