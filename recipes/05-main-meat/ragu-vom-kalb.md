## Summary

Slow oven-braised veal ragu for pappardelle.

## Ingredients

- 800 g veal (stew cut)
- 2 carrots
- 1 onion
- 3 celery stalks
- 500 ml veal stock
- 700 g passata
- 8 tbsp olive oil
- 3 bay leaves
- 1 pinch sugar
- salt and black pepper
- 400 g pappardelle
- parsley

## Preparation

1. Heat oven to 160 C. Cube and salt veal.
2. Brown veal in batches in olive oil and transfer to roasting dish.
3. Brown diced vegetables, then add stock, passata, bay leaves, sugar, salt, and pepper.
4. Pour sauce over veal, cover, and braise about 2.5 hours. Uncover halfway.
5. Shred veal lightly with forks in the sauce.
6. Cook pappardelle al dente and mix with ragu, loosening with a little pasta water.
7. Finish with chopped parsley.
