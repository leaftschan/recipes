## Summary

Spiced grilled beef koefte with herbs and a long marination.

## Ingredients

- 3 slices toast bread, diced
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 3 garlic cloves, finely chopped
- 1 bunch flat-leaf parsley, finely chopped
- 1/2 bunch mint, finely chopped
- 1 kg minced beef
- salt and black pepper
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp cardamom powder
- 1 tsp coriander seeds
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp allspice
- 1 tsp sweet paprika
- 2 tbsp olive oil

## Preparation

1. Dice toast bread.
2. Chop onion, garlic, parsley, and mint finely.
3. Mix beef with toast, onion, garlic, herbs, salt, and pepper.
4. Add egg, baking powder, and spices.
5. Knead by hand until compact and uniform.
6. Cover and marinate in fridge about 24 hours.
7. Shape into about 20 rolls with wet hands.
8. Brush lightly with oil and grill around 200 C for about 10 minutes.
