## Summary

Warm flatbread meal with seasoned minced beef, herb yogurt, and tomato.

## Ingredients

- 500 g minced beef
- salt and black pepper
- 250 g yogurt
- coriander, chopped
- dill, chopped
- spring onions, chopped
- tomatoes, thinly sliced
- pita or doener bread
- optional: arganiok or sriracha

## Preparation

1. Brown minced beef in a pan and season with salt and pepper.
2. Chop coriander, dill, and spring onions.
3. Mix herbs and spring onions with yogurt; season lightly.
4. Warm pita/doener bread.
5. Fill bread with minced beef.
6. Add yogurt sauce and tomato slices.
7. Serve with arganiok or sriracha if desired.
