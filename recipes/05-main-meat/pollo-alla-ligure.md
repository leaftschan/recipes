## Summary

Ligurian chicken braised with olives, pine nuts, white wine, tomatoes, and potatoes.

## Ingredients

- 1 chicken (about 1.5 kg), cut in pieces
- 20 olives
- 2 garlic cloves
- 30 g toasted pine nuts
- 200 ml dry white wine
- 200 g cherry tomatoes
- 30 ml olive oil
- 300 g potatoes, wedged
- 400 ml chicken broth
- 1 sprig marjoram
- 1 sprig rosemary
- basil and parsley, chopped
- salt
- black pepper

## Preparation

1. Heat oven to 180 C fan.
2. Brown half the chicken in olive oil. Add garlic and herbs briefly, deglaze with half the wine, transfer to baking dish.
3. Brown remaining chicken, add tomatoes and remaining wine, reduce briefly, and add to dish.
4. Add olives and pine nuts. Deglaze pan with broth and pour over chicken.
5. Bake 30 minutes. Add potato wedges and mix.
6. Bake 30 minutes more until chicken and potatoes are tender.
7. Finish with basil and parsley.
