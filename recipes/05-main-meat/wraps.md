## Summary

Build-your-own wraps with spiced filling, sauteed vegetables, and fresh toppings.

## Ingredients

### Protein and spice base

- 300 g minced meat
- 1 onion
- 1 spring onion
- tomato paste
- cumin
- caraway
- ground coriander seeds
- chili

### Vegetable filling

- 1 pepperoni/pepper
- 1 zucchini
- 2 tomatoes
- small can corn
- bouillon powder

### Toppings and wraps

- 150 ml sour half cream
- coriander, chopped
- sriracha
- duruem wraps

## Preparation

1. Finely chop onion and chili and saute.
2. Add minced meat, then tomato paste and spices.
3. Add chopped spring onion at the end.
4. Cut zucchini and pepperoni/pepper into short sticks and saute separately.
5. Season vegetables with bouillon powder and ground coriander.
6. Slice tomatoes and set out with corn, cream, sriracha, and chopped coriander.
7. Warm wraps in oven.
8. Assemble wraps at the table.
