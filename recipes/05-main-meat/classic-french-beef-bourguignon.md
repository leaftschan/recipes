## Summary

Slow-braised beef stew with red wine, bacon, mushrooms, and pearl onions.

## Ingredients

- 1.8 kg beef chuck, cut into large cubes
- 170 g bacon, diced
- 750 ml dry red wine
- 500 ml beef stock
- 2 carrots, sliced
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 tbsp flour
- 300 g pearl onions
- 300 g mushrooms, halved
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 bouquet garni
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Brown bacon in a Dutch oven, remove, and keep the fat in the pot.
2. Pat beef dry, season, and brown in batches in the bacon fat and a little oil.
3. Add onion, carrots, and garlic and cook until lightly softened.
4. Stir in tomato paste and flour for 1 minute.
5. Return bacon and beef, then add wine, stock, and bouquet garni.
6. Cover and braise in a 160 C oven for about 3 hours until beef is tender.
7. Saute pearl onions and mushrooms in butter until golden and tender.
8. Stir onions and mushrooms into the stew, simmer uncovered 10 minutes, and adjust seasoning.
