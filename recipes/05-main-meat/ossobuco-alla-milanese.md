## Summary

Braised veal shanks with gremolata and saffron risotto.

## Ingredients

### Ossobuco

- 4 veal shanks, 3 to 4 cm thick
- 40 g butter
- 50 ml olive oil
- 75 g onion, diced
- 75 g carrot, diced
- 75 g celery, diced
- 1 garlic clove
- 200 ml dry white wine
- 400 ml beef broth
- 400 g peeled tomatoes
- thyme, rosemary, sage, bay leaf
- 50 g flour
- salt and pepper

### Gremolata

- parsley
- 1/2 garlic clove
- zest of 1 lemon

## Preparation

1. Dust veal shanks in flour and brown in butter/oil.
2. Saute onion, carrot, celery, and garlic in the same pan.
3. Return meat, add herbs, wine, broth, and tomatoes.
4. Transfer to casserole, cover, and braise at 165 C fan for about 1.5 to 2 hours.
5. Finely chop parsley and garlic and mix with lemon zest for gremolata.
6. Serve ossobuco hot and finish with gremolata.
