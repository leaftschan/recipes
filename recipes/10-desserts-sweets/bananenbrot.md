## Summary

Soft banana loaf that works as breakfast, snack, or dessert.

## Ingredients

### Batter

- 3 ripe bananas
- 220 g flour
- 120 g sugar
- 100 g butter or neutral oil
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 pinch salt

### Optional

- 80 g chocolate chips
- 60 g chopped nuts
- 1 tsp cinnamon

## Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 175 C and grease the loaf pan.
2. Mash bananas and whisk with eggs, fat, and sugar.
3. Fold in flour, baking powder, and salt until just combined.
4. Add optional ingredients.
5. Bake for 50 to 60 minutes until a skewer comes out mostly clean.
6. Cool before slicing.
