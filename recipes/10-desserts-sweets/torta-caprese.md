## Summary

Flourless chocolate-nut cake from Capri.

## Ingredients

- 180 g butter, room temperature
- 180 g powdered sugar, divided
- 90 g egg yolk
- 150 g egg white
- 90 g ground almonds
- 90 g ground hazelnuts
- 40 g chopped hazelnuts
- 180 g chopped dark chocolate (about 55%)
- 15 g cocoa powder
- 30 g cornstarch
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp vanilla paste
- 1/4 tsp salt
- extra powdered sugar to finish

## Preparation

1. Heat oven to 175 C and line a 22 cm tin.
2. Mix almonds, hazelnuts, chopped nuts, chocolate, cocoa, cornstarch, and baking powder.
3. Beat butter with half the powdered sugar, then add salt, vanilla, and yolks.
4. Whip egg whites with remaining powdered sugar to soft peaks.
5. Fold egg whites and dry mix gently into butter mixture.
6. Fill tin and bake 40 to 45 minutes.
7. Cool and dust with powdered sugar.
