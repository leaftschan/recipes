## Summary

Moist yogurt loaf cake scented with lemon zest and poppy seeds.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 0.5 cup neutral oil
- zest of 2 lemons
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tbsp poppy seeds
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1 pinch salt

## Preparation

1. Heat oven to 180 C and grease a loaf pan.
2. Whisk yogurt, sugar, eggs, oil, zest, and lemon juice.
3. Fold in flour, baking powder, salt, and poppy seeds just until combined.
4. Pour into pan and bake 40 to 45 minutes until a skewer comes out clean.
5. Cool 10 minutes in pan, then unmold and cool fully.
