## Summary

Swiss-style sweet fried bread slices served with homemade applesauce.

## Ingredients

### Fotzelschnitten

- about 1 kg stale butter braid bread (or similar)
- milk
- at least 6 eggs
- sugar
- frying fat
- cinnamon sugar for serving

### Applesauce

- tart apples (for example Braeburn)
- sugar
- a little water (up to about 1 dl)
- optional: fresh ginger

## Preparation

1. Peel apples, core, and cut into wedges.
2. Cook with a little water and sugar, covered, at low heat for at least 20 minutes.
3. Optional: finely dice ginger, caramelize briefly, then add apples and water.
4. Crush apples lightly during cooking to desired texture.
5. Slice bread into finger-thick slices.
6. Whisk eggs with about 5 dl milk.
7. Dip bread slices in egg mixture and fry until golden.
8. Serve with cinnamon sugar and applesauce.
