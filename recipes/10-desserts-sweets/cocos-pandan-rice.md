## Summary

Fragrant coconut pandan rice with light sweetness.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup basmati rice
- about 250 g coconut milk
- water (to reach roughly 2 cups total liquid)
- 2 pandan leaves, knotted
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt

## Preparation

1. Wash rice well.
2. Combine rice, coconut milk, water, sugar, salt, and pandan leaves in a pot.
3. Bring to a boil with lid on.
4. Reduce heat to minimum and cook about 10 minutes.
5. Turn off heat and let rice rest covered.
6. Remove pandan leaves and fluff before serving.
