## Summary

Rustic free-form tart with sweet strawberries and tangy rhubarb.

## Ingredients

- 1 pie dough disc
- 250 g strawberries, hulled and halved
- 250 g rhubarb, sliced
- 90 g sugar
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1 tbsp coarse sugar

## Preparation

1. Heat oven to 200 C and line a baking sheet.
2. Toss strawberries, rhubarb, sugar, cornstarch, and vanilla.
3. Roll dough into a rough circle and transfer to the sheet.
4. Spoon filling in the center, leaving a 4 to 5 cm border.
5. Fold edges over the fruit and brush dough with beaten egg.
6. Sprinkle coarse sugar and bake 35 to 40 minutes until deeply golden.
7. Cool slightly before slicing.
