## Summary

Classic tiramisu with espresso, mascarpone cream, and cocoa.

## Ingredients

- 500 g mascarpone
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 tbsp sugar
- 2 cold espresso shots
- 4 cl marsala
- 150 g savoiardi (ladyfingers)
- 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder

## Preparation

1. Beat yolks with sugar until pale and creamy.
2. Fold mascarpone into the yolk mixture until smooth.
3. Mix espresso with marsala in a shallow bowl.
4. Dip half the ladyfingers briefly and line a dish.
5. Spread half the cream, repeat with remaining ladyfingers and cream.
6. Chill at least 4 hours.
7. Dust with cocoa before serving.
