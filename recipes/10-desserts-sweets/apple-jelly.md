## Summary

Clear apple jelly with a bright fruit flavor and gentle sweetness.

## Ingredients

- 2 kg apples, roughly chopped
- 1.5 l water
- sugar (about 750 g per liter of strained juice)
- 2 tbsp lemon juice

## Preparation

1. Simmer apples and water for 45 to 60 minutes until very soft.
2. Strain through a jelly bag overnight without pressing.
3. Measure juice and add sugar based on volume.
4. Return to a pot with lemon juice and boil until setting point is reached.
5. Pour into sterilized jars, seal, and cool.
