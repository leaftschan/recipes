## Summary

Layered strawberry tiramisu with mascarpone cream, orange juice, and limoncello.

## Ingredients

- 500 g strawberries
- 500 g mascarpone
- 100 g powdered sugar
- 200 g ladyfingers
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 egg whites
- 3 cups orange juice
- 2 tbsp limoncello
- pistachio brittle for topping

## Preparation

1. Wash strawberries, reserve some for garnish, and slice the rest.
2. Beat yolks with sugar, fold in limoncello and mascarpone.
3. Whip egg whites to stiff peaks and fold into the cream.
4. Layer cream, briefly soaked ladyfingers (orange juice), and strawberries in a dish.
5. Repeat layers and finish with cream.
6. Chill overnight.
7. Top with pistachio brittle and fresh strawberries.
