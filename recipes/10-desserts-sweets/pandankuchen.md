## Summary

Soft pandan-coconut loaf cake with whipped egg whites for lift.

## Ingredients

### Pandan water

- pandan leaves
- about 1 dl water

### Batter

- 6 eggs, separated
- 1 pinch salt
- 60 g sugar
- 80 ml pandan water
- 80 ml sunflower oil
- 200 ml coconut milk
- 200 g flour
- 1 tsp baking powder

## Preparation

1. For pandan water, wash pandan leaves, cut into small pieces, blend with water, and strain.
2. Preheat oven to 175 C.
3. Beat egg whites with salt; add about half the sugar just before stiff peaks form.
4. Beat egg yolks with remaining sugar and pandan water until foamy.
5. Mix in oil, coconut milk, flour, and baking powder.
6. Fold egg whites into batter gently.
7. Transfer to greased loaf pan.
8. Bake about 45 minutes.
