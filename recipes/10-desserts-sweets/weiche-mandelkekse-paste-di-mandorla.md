## Summary

Soft Sicilian almond cookies with orange zest.

## Ingredients

- 250 g ground almonds
- 200 g sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 2 tsp honey
- zest of 1 organic orange
- 3 tsp powdered sugar for coating

## Preparation

1. Mix almonds, sugar, egg whites, honey, and orange zest into a sticky dough.
2. Chill dough 30 minutes.
3. Heat oven to 180 C top and bottom heat.
4. Form walnut-sized balls, roll in powdered sugar, and place on lined tray.
5. Pinch each ball lightly at the top.
6. Bake about 10 minutes until lightly golden.
