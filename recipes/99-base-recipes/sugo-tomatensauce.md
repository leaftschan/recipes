## Summary

Slow-cooked tomato sugo with onion, garlic, oregano, and basil.

## Ingredients

- 3 cans peeled tomatoes
- 80 g olive oil
- 1 small onion, finely diced
- 3 garlic cloves, lightly crushed
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- pinch of sugar
- 6 basil sprigs or leaves
- salt to taste

## Preparation

1. Heat olive oil and saute onion until translucent.
2. Crush or mill tomatoes and add to pot.
3. Add garlic, oregano, and sugar.
4. Simmer gently 1 to 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
5. Remove garlic near the end.
6. Season with salt and finish with basil and a little olive oil.
