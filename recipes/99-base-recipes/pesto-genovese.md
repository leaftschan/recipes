## Summary

Classic basil pesto made in a mortar.

## Ingredients

- 1 pinch fine sea salt
- 1 garlic clove
- 50 g pine nuts
- 100 g basil leaves
- 50 g grated parmesan
- 150 ml extra virgin olive oil

## Preparation

1. Pound garlic and salt in a mortar.
2. Add pine nuts in portions and crush.
3. Add basil gradually and pound to a paste.
4. Add parmesan and mix until creamy.
5. Pour in olive oil slowly while stirring or pounding.
6. Use immediately or refrigerate covered with a thin layer of oil.
