## Summary

Tomato sauce with sweet potato, carrot, onion, and garlic.

## Ingredients

- 1 large can peeled tomatoes
- 2 onions
- garlic
- 4 carrots
- 2 sweet potatoes
- coriander
- salt and pepper
- water

## Preparation

1. Dice sweet potatoes and brown briefly in a pot.
2. Add onions, carrots, and garlic.
3. Add canned tomatoes.
4. Fill tomato can with water and add.
5. Simmer until vegetables are fully soft.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Add coriander to finish if desired.

## Notes

- Pairs well with cumin rice.
- Blend smooth for a creamier sauce.
