## Summary

Classic Italian egg-free pasta dough made from semola and water.

## Ingredients

- 400 g semola di grano duro (fine durum wheat semolina)
- 200 ml water
- 1/2 tsp salt
- a few extra drops of water, only if needed

## Preparation

1. Place semola on the work surface in a mound and form a well in the center.
2. Add salt to the well and gradually knead in the water.
3. Knead by hand (or with a mixer) until everything comes together.
4. If the dough is too firm, add water drop by drop.
5. Continue kneading until smooth and elastic but not sticky.
6. Shape into a ball, wrap in plastic, and rest for 30 minutes.
7. Roll out with a pasta machine or rolling pin and cut/shape as desired.
8. Cook in plenty of boiling salted water until the pasta floats and is al dente, usually 2 to 3 minutes for fresh pasta.

## Notes

- Do not leave the dough exposed to air for long, or it becomes brittle.
- Fresh egg-free pasta can be dried completely and stored airtight.
- You can also freeze shaped fresh pasta directly and cook from frozen.
