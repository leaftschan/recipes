## Summary

Pan-fried tuna patties with herbs and lemon.

## Ingredients

### Patties

- 2 cans tuna in water, drained well
- 1 egg
- 60 g breadcrumbs
- 1 small onion, finely diced
- 1 tbsp parsley, chopped
- zest of 1 lemon
- salt and pepper

### For frying

- 2 tbsp oil

## Preparation

1. Mix all patty ingredients in a bowl.
2. Rest mixture for 5 minutes so breadcrumbs hydrate.
3. Shape into 8 small patties.
4. Fry in hot oil for 3 to 4 minutes per side until golden.
5. Serve with salad, yogurt dip, or lemon wedges.
