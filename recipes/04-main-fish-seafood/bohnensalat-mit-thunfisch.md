## Summary

Ligurian bean and tuna salad with white and green beans in herb vinaigrette.

## Ingredients

- 200 g dried white beans
- 1 bay leaf
- 3 garlic cloves
- 200 g green beans
- 300 g tuna in oil, drained
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1 red onion, thinly sliced
- 2 anchovy fillets, optional
- 3 tbsp white balsamic vinegar
- 6 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp mustard
- 1 tsp sugar
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley
- 2 tbsp basil
- salt
- black pepper

## Preparation

1. Soak white beans overnight. Simmer with bay leaf and 2 garlic cloves until tender, then cool.
2. Cook green beans in salted water until just tender, then rinse cool.
3. Whisk vinegar, mustard, sugar, olive oil, and remaining garlic into a dressing.
4. Combine beans, tuna, onion, tomatoes, and optional anchovy.
5. Add dressing, parsley, and basil. Mix well and let stand briefly before serving.
