## Summary

Tomato tuna salad with coriander, balsamic, and olive oil.

## Ingredients

- 500 g ripe cherry tomatoes
- 1 can tuna in brine
- plenty of coriander, finely chopped
- balsamic vinegar
- olive oil
- bouillon powder
- black pepper

## Preparation

1. Halve cherry tomatoes.
2. Open tuna can fully and press tuna with lid to drain liquid.
3. Add tuna to tomatoes and break into smaller pieces with a fork.
4. Season with pepper and a little bouillon powder.
5. Dress with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
6. Mix in finely chopped coriander.
