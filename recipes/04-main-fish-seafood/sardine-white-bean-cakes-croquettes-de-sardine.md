## Summary

Pan-fried sardine and white bean patties with lemon and herbs.

## Ingredients

- 2 cans sardines in olive oil, drained
- 250 g cooked white beans
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp breadcrumbs
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tbsp parsley, chopped
- black pepper
- olive oil for frying

## Preparation

1. Mash beans and sardines together in a bowl.
2. Mix in egg, breadcrumbs, Dijon, lemon juice, parsley, and pepper.
3. Form small patties and chill 10 minutes.
4. Fry in olive oil over medium heat until golden on both sides.
5. Serve with a green salad or yogurt sauce.
