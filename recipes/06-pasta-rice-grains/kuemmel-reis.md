## Summary

Simple rice side dish finished with caraway.

## Ingredients

- 250 g rice
- 1 tsp caraway seeds
- water

## Preparation

1. Cook rice using about double the amount of water.
2. Drain if needed.
3. Mix in caraway seeds while rice is still hot.
