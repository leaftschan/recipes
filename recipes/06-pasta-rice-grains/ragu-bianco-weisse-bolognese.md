## Summary

Tomato-free bolognese style meat sauce with pancetta, soffritto, white wine, and broth.

## Ingredients

- 300 g mixed ground meat
- 50 g pancetta, diced
- 1 small onion
- 1 celery stalk
- 1 small carrot
- 1 garlic clove
- 6 sprigs thyme
- 100 ml white wine
- 200 ml vegetable broth
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 20 g cold butter
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Finely chop onion, celery, carrot, and garlic.
2. Saute vegetables with pancetta and thyme in olive oil until soft.
3. Increase heat, add ground meat, and brown thoroughly.
4. Season, deglaze with white wine, and reduce.
5. Add broth and simmer 10 to 15 minutes.
6. Stir in cold butter at the end to bind the sauce.
