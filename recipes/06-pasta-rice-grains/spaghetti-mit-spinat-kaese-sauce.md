## Summary

Spaghetti with quick spinach-cream-cheese sauce, egg, and optional pine nuts.

## Ingredients

- 500 g spaghetti
- 300 g baby spinach
- garlic, pressed
- nutmeg
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- 160 g full-fat cream cheese
- olive oil
- salt and pepper
- optional: pine nuts

## Preparation

1. Cook spaghetti al dente.
2. Drain pasta over spinach so spinach wilts from heat.
3. Reserve about 1 dl pasta water.
4. Return pasta and spinach to pot and keep warm with lid.
5. Mix pasta water with about 2 tbsp olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
6. Pour over pasta.
7. Fold in cream cheese gradually with two forks.
8. Top with chopped eggs and optional pine nuts.

## Notes

- Add grated cheese if you want a richer sauce.
- Extra pasta water helps loosen the sauce.
