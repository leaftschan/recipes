## Summary

Creamy pasta bake with ham cubes, herbs, sbrinz, and egg-cream custard.

## Ingredients

- 300 g hoernli pasta
- parsley, finely chopped
- 200 g ham cubes
- 100 g grated sbrinz
- 4 dl milk
- 3 dl full cream
- 4 eggs
- salt and pepper

## Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 200 C.
2. Cook pasta in salted water until al dente.
3. Drain and place in prepared casserole dish.
4. Mix in parsley, ham cubes, and sbrinz.
5. Whisk milk, cream, eggs, salt, and pepper.
6. Pour custard over pasta mixture.
7. Bake about 50 minutes in lower half of oven.

## Notes

- Let rest a few minutes before slicing.
- Can be adapted with vegetables instead of ham.
