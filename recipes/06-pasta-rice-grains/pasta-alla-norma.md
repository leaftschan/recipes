## Summary

Sicilian pasta with fried aubergine, tomato sauce, basil, and ricotta.

## Ingredients

- 400 g pasta (spaghetti or penne)
- 600 g tomatoes
- 2 aubergines
- 100 g ricotta (preferably ricotta salata)
- 1 garlic clove
- 2 tbsp chopped basil
- 8 tbsp olive oil
- salt

## Preparation

1. Slice aubergines, salt lightly, and rest about 1 hour to draw out moisture.
2. Saute garlic in olive oil, add tomatoes, basil, and salt, and simmer 15 minutes.
3. Fry aubergine slices in a second pan until golden. Cut most into strips.
4. Add aubergine strips to tomato sauce.
5. Cook pasta al dente and toss briefly with sauce.
6. Plate and finish with remaining aubergine and crumbled ricotta.
