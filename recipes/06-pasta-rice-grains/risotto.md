## Summary

Basic risotto with onion, chili, bouillon, parmesan, and optional add-ins.

## Ingredients

- 1 onion
- 1 cup risotto rice
- 1 chili
- bouillon
- grated parmesan
- optional: a little white wine
- choose one add-in, for example asparagus or mushrooms

## Preparation

1. Finely chop onion and chili and saute in olive oil.
2. Add risotto rice and toast briefly.
3. Add chosen extra ingredient now if it benefits from cooking with the rice.
4. Deglaze with a splash of white wine, or use water.
5. Add bouillon until rice is just covered.
6. Stir regularly.
7. Each time the liquid is absorbed, add more bouillon.
8. When rice is cooked and creamy, remove from heat and stir in parmesan.

## Notes

- Texture should stay creamy, not dry.
- Finish with butter if you want a richer result.
