## Summary

Creamy Italian polenta with butter and parmesan.

## Ingredients

- 250 g fine cornmeal
- 1 l water
- 1 splash milk
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp grated parmesan

## Preparation

1. Bring salted water to a boil.
2. Add milk and reduce heat.
3. Whisk in cornmeal slowly.
4. Cook on low heat about 30 to 45 minutes, stirring often.
5. Stir in butter and parmesan at the end.
6. Serve creamy, or cool and slice for pan-frying later.
