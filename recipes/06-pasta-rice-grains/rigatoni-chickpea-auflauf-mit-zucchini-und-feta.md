## Summary

Oven-baked rigatoni with chickpeas, zucchini, tomato, and feta.

## Ingredients

- 1 small zucchini
- 1 small onion
- 1 garlic clove
- 100 g rigatoni
- 1 can chickpeas (about 250 g)
- 1 can peeled tomatoes (about 250 g)
- 1/2 pack feta
- salt and black pepper
- cayenne pepper

## Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 180 C, top and bottom heat.
2. Cook rigatoni about 2 minutes less than package time.
3. Rinse chickpeas well.
4. Saute onion, then add garlic and zucchini.
5. Season with salt, pepper, and cayenne.
6. Add chickpeas, then tomatoes, then rigatoni.
7. Transfer to casserole dish and top with feta.
8. Bake about 40 minutes.
