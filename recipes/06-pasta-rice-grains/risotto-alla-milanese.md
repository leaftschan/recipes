## Summary

Cremy saffron risotto in Milan style.

## Ingredients

- 1 l beef broth
- 2 shallots, finely chopped
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- 300 g carnaroli rice
- 100 ml dry white wine
- 25 saffron threads (or 1 tsp saffron powder)
- 3 tbsp water
- 4 tbsp grated parmesan
- 100 g cold butter
- salt

## Preparation

1. Soak saffron threads in 3 tbsp water for several hours.
2. Keep broth hot.
3. Saute shallots in olive oil, add rice, and toast briefly.
4. Deglaze with white wine and reduce.
5. Add hot broth gradually, stirring often, until rice is nearly done.
6. Stir in saffron and soaking liquid, finish cooking to al dente.
7. Off heat, stir in parmesan and cold butter until creamy.
