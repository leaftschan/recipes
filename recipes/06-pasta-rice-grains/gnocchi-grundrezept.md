## Summary

Classic Italian potato gnocchi.

## Ingredients

- 800 g floury potatoes
- 300 g flour
- 1 egg
- salt

## Preparation

1. Boil potatoes, peel while warm, and press through a potato ricer.
2. Add flour, egg, and salt and knead briefly into a compact dough.
3. Divide into portions and roll into finger-thick ropes.
4. Cut into 2 cm pieces.
5. Roll each piece over a fork or gnocchi board to form ridges.
6. Cook in gently simmering salted water until gnocchi float.
7. Remove with a skimmer and serve with sauce.
