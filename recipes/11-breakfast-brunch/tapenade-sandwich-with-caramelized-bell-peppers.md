## Summary

Mediterranean-style sandwich with olive tapenade and sweet peppers.

## Ingredients

- 1 baguette or 4 sandwich rolls
- 3 bell peppers, sliced
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 4 tbsp black olive tapenade
- 120 g fresh goat cheese or cream cheese
- black pepper

## Preparation

1. Cook peppers and onion in olive oil over medium-low heat until soft and caramelized.
2. Slice bread and spread with tapenade and goat cheese.
3. Add warm pepper-onion mixture and season with black pepper.
4. Close sandwiches and serve warm or room temperature.
