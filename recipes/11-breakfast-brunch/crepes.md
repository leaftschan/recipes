## Summary

Classic thin crepes with a smooth batter and quick pan-frying.

## Ingredients

- 250 g white flour
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 3 eggs
- 500 ml milk
- 2 tbsp melted butter
- butter for the pan

## Preparation

1. Whisk flour and salt in a bowl.
2. Add eggs and about half of the milk, then whisk until smooth.
3. Stir in remaining milk and melted butter. Rest for 10 to 15 minutes.
4. Heat a lightly buttered skillet over medium heat.
5. Pour in a thin layer of batter, swirl, and cook until lightly golden on both sides.
6. Repeat with remaining batter and serve sweet or savory.
