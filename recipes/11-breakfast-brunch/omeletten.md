## Summary

Basic crepe-style omelette batter for thin Swiss omelettes.

## Ingredients

- 600 g flour
- 2 tsp salt
- at least 6 large eggs
- 100 g melted cold butter
- 6 dl milk
- 6 dl water

## Preparation

1. Mix flour and salt.
2. Whisk eggs, butter, milk, and water together.
3. Add liquid mixture to flour, not the other way around.
4. Whisk until smooth and lump-free.
5. Let batter rest at least 30 minutes.
6. Cook thin portions in a nonstick pan until golden.
