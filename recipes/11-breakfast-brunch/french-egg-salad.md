## Summary

Simple creamy egg salad with Dijon mustard and fresh herbs.

## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3 tbsp mayonnaise
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp white wine vinegar
- 1 tbsp chives, chopped
- 1 tbsp parsley, chopped
- salt and black pepper

## Preparation

1. Boil eggs for 9 to 10 minutes, then cool in cold water and peel.
2. Chop eggs and place in a bowl.
3. Mix mayonnaise, Dijon, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
4. Fold dressing and herbs into eggs.
5. Chill briefly and serve with bread or greens.
